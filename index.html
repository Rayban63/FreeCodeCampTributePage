Chip Foose


American automobile designer


BIOGRAPHY


  • 1963 - Chip was born on October 13, in Santa Barbara, California
  • 1982 - He attended the Art Center College of Design
  • 1984 - Chip, started working at the Clenet Coachworks
  • 1988 - He went back to finish his education at the Art Center College of Design
  • 1990 - Chip, graduated
  • 1990 - Chip, worked full-time for Sterenberger Design and part-time for Boyd Coddington
  • 1998 - Chip, left his position and with his wife Lynne started his own automotive and product design company called Foose Design
  • 2003 - Chip, gained more exposure, as a result of a TLC documentary on his design and creation of a modified 2002 Ford Thunderbird
  • 2006 - Chip, launched a line of die cast replicas of many of his famous designs
  • 2007 - Chip, began limited production (50 vehicles in total) of the Hemisfear. Also known as the Foose Coupe
